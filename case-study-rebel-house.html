Overview

Rebel House Utah, a boutique indoor cycling studio, needed a complete brand refresh to better reflect their energetic, community-focused atmosphere. This unsolicited redesign project reimagines their digital platform to enhance class booking, strengthen community connections, and boost member motivation. The redesigned website serves as the foundation for a scalable ecosystem that could expand into a mobile app. Through strategic UX improvements and cohesive brand identity, this concept bridges the gap between Rebel House's inspiring studio atmosphere and their online presence.

Introduction

Let's Get Physical (and Digital)

Rebel House creates transformative fitness experiences through their signature RIDE, REFINE, RIOT, and REHAB classes. However, their digital presence wasn't capturing the bold, empowering energy that members experience in-studio. New users felt intimidated navigating the platform, existing members had no way to track progress or engage with the community online, and the studio lacked tools for referrals and local partnerships—all missed opportunities for growth.

This redesign addresses these critical pain points by creating a warm, intuitive digital experience that mirrors the studio's inclusive atmosphere. The goal was to reduce friction for new users, provide meaningful engagement tools for existing members, and give Rebel House a platform that supports retention, referrals, and community building while standing out in a competitive market.

Process

Brand research & discovery

I conducted extensive research through Rebel House's social media channels, review pages on Facebook and Google, and competitor analysis to understand what made the studio special to its community. Through synthesizing user feedback and identifying patterns, I discovered that members valued three core elements: the bold, empowering energy of instructors, the inclusive community atmosphere, and the distinctive identity of each class type (RIDE, REFINE, RIOT, REHAB). This research revealed that while the in-person experience was exceptional, the digital presence felt disconnected from these brand values, creating an opportunity to build a cohesive identity that would resonate across all touchpoints.

Research Findings & User Insights

Key Brand Attributes: Empowering fitness that's accessible and confidence-building, energetic classes with a motivating atmosphere, inclusive space welcoming all fitness levels, and a community-driven approach that builds connections beyond the bike.

Solution

Brand identity development

The brand identity evolved from understanding Rebel House's unique position in the boutique fitness landscape. They are not just another cycling studio, but a holistic wellness community. I developed a visual language that balances the studio's high-energy, bold personality with warmth and approachability. The identity system emphasizes movement, progress, and connection while maintaining the distinctive character of each class offering. This foundation ensures that whether a member is booking their first class or celebrating their 100th ride, the brand consistently communicates empowerment, inclusivity, and transformation.

Color Palette & Typography System

The visual identity centers on an earthy color palette (#333E21, #32373F, #BA9F8F, #DFCBB5) that creates warmth and inclusivity, a deliberate shift from the previous site's stark black, white, and cool grays. Syncopate serves as the heading typeface, with its wide-set, contemporary design suggesting motion and expansion that aligns with themes of growth and progress, while Inter provides clean, readable body text that feels modern yet accessible. Movement is central to the visual language through blurred horizontal gradients, animated hand-drawn lines, and bold directional arrows that reflect an active, evolving community. This minimalist approach reduces visual noise while maintaining energy, helping users feel both inspired and at ease.

Implementation

Website redesign & user experience

The website redesign prioritizes intuitive navigation and frictionless user flows, starting with a reimagined information architecture that guides new users seamlessly from discovery to their first class booking. The homepage immediately communicates value through dynamic visuals and clear pathways to key actions—Book, Buy, and Learn More—while showcasing the four signature class experiences. Each class type (RIDE, REFINE, RIOT, REHAB) receives dedicated pages with rich descriptions, instructor spotlights, and visual storytelling that helps users find their perfect fit. The booking flow was streamlined to reduce steps from five to three, while membership pages clearly compare options with transparent pricing and benefits.

Homepage Redesign - Desktop View

Beyond basic functionality, the redesign introduces features that build community and drive engagement. A personal dashboard allows members to track class history, view progress toward goals, and earn achievement badges that celebrate milestones. The platform integrates social proof through testimonials and success stories, while a referral system with local partnership coupons encourages organic growth. Enhanced content includes trainer video introductions and calendar integration that makes scheduling effortless. These features work together to create a digital experience that not only serves functional needs but also reinforces the sense of belonging that makes Rebel House special.
