TRI-N SCHOOL AND OFFICE SUPPLIES

E-commerce platform for school and office supplies serving retail and institutional clients

Industry

E-commerce & Retail

My Role

UX/UI Designer, E-commerce Design, Information Architecture

Timeline

2-3 weeks

Team

1 designer, 2 developers, 1 product manager

Overview

Tri-N School and Office Supplies needed an e-commerce website to expand their business online and serve both retail and institutional clients. The challenge was to create an intuitive platform for a diverse product catalog, catering to both individual buyers and bulk orders while organizing a large inventory effectively with easy-to-use search, filtering, and a smooth checkout process.

Tri-N E-commerce Platform Hero Image
Introduction

E-commerce challenge for dual market segments

The business needed to transition from physical retail to online sales while serving two distinct customer segments with different needs: individual consumers making small purchases and institutional buyers requiring bulk orders with specific procurement workflows.

Key challenges included managing diverse product categories from pens to furniture, accommodating different purchasing behaviors and needs, creating efficient inventory browsing for thousands of SKUs, and implementing flexible checkout for both small and bulk orders.

User Segments & Business Analysis
Process

Understanding buyer behavior & needs

Conducted research with both retail customers and institutional procurement officers to understand their shopping patterns, pain points, and decision-making processes. Analyzed competitor e-commerce platforms and identified opportunities for differentiation.

User Research & Journey Mapping

Key Insights: Retail customers prioritize quick product discovery and checkout, institutional buyers need bulk pricing and order management features, search and filtering are critical for large inventories, and mobile optimization is essential for on-the-go ordering.

Information Architecture & Site Structure
Solution

Dual-mode e-commerce design

Designed a flexible e-commerce platform with two distinct shopping modes: retail mode for individual buyers with streamlined browsing and quick checkout, and institutional mode featuring bulk pricing, quote requests, and account management. The system adapts based on customer type while maintaining consistent brand experience.

Product Catalog & Navigation System

Implemented advanced filtering by category, price, brand, and specifications, smart search with autocomplete and suggestions, quick add-to-cart functionality, and comparison tools for evaluating multiple products.

Search & Filter Interface
Implementation

Checkout & order management

Created streamlined checkout flow accommodating both retail and bulk purchases. For institutional buyers, added features like saved carts, purchase order integration, approval workflows, and invoice management. Retail customers benefit from guest checkout, saved addresses, and multiple payment options.

Checkout Flow & Payment Interface

Mobile-responsive design ensures seamless browsing and purchasing across devices. Quick reorder functionality, order tracking, and customer account management features enhance the post-purchase experience.

Mobile Experience & Account Dashboard
Design Impact

Business growth & user satisfaction

- Successfully launched e-commerce platform serving both retail and institutional customer segments

- Efficient product discovery through advanced search and filtering reduces time to purchase

- Dual-mode shopping experience accommodates different buyer needs without compromising usability

- Mobile-optimized design enables convenient on-the-go ordering and reordering
