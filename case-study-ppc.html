The Challenge

M. G. Welding & Fences needed a site redesign to communicate the company’s services better. Its current design feels plain and lacks visual impact to showcase their welding and fencing services and attract new residential and commercial clients.

The challenge:

  • Introduce assets that better represent welding and metalwork.
  • Update color scheme to reflect logo colors, craftsmanship, and reliability.
  • Replace generic fonts with a bold, industrial-style typeface.
  • Ensure CTAs stand out clearly across all sections of the site.
  • Enhance user engagement by making site more interactive

My Approach

  • The redesign focuses on energizing the site's existing plain white neutral color aesthetic with a more dynamic and industrial feel.
  • I introduced welding-themed visual elements like subtle spark effects and metal textures to strengthen the brand's identity using CSS
  • Buttons were redesigned with bold gradients, glow effects, and 3D interactions to draw attention and improve engagement.
  • Typography has been upgraded to strong, industrial fonts (Bebas Neue and Open Sans) for better readability and visual impact.
  • Animations were refined to ensure smoother performance—moved from containers to individual elements to prevent conflicts with SVG lines.
  • Additional UI polish, including card hover effects, glowing form fields, and accented testimonials, adds a professional, cohesive finish that enhances both usability and aesthetic appeal.
