The Challenge

Clean Solutions USA needed a complete website overhaul to better serve their dual business model: power washing services and holiday lighting installations.

Client Requirements

The client wanted the overall appearance and User Experience (UX) of the site to emphasize professionalism, quick service access, and customer trust. The design should incorporate clear visual hierarchy, strong branding elements, and intuitive input fields that make interaction seamless and confidence-building for users.

Design Direction

Given the cheerful bucket mascot, the site should feel:

  • Playful but professional - fun like the mascot, trustworthy for business
  • Bright and clean - lots of white space, fresh colors
  • Approachable - rounded corners, friendly typography
  • Energetic - vibrant blues, maybe some orange accents

The Solution

Colors

Color Palette Transformation

  • Replaced the dark background with a clean white or very light cream background
  • Added vibrant accent colors like bright blues and energetic orange
  • Used high contrast - dark text on light backgrounds for better readability

Key Improvements

The design incorporates several key visual and structural elements:

  • Prominent Headings and Branding: The site features the main heading "Homepage" and uses strong taglines like "Shine Brighter with Every Clean" and "A Fresh Approach to Service", suggesting a modern, professional brand ethos. I added a CSS shadow effect to make the headings more prominent.
  • Strategic CTA Design: I chose a clear vibrant color for the call-to-action buttons. This creates perfect contrast with the blues, feels energetic and action-oriented, and is commonly used by service companies because it conveys urgency and friendliness. I also added a hover effect adding a subtle lift and glow that makes the button feel interactive without being too flashy.
  • Visual Media: The design includes video components, located near the top of the page
  • Structured Layout: Content is organized into clear sections detailing service benefits, areas served, and a booking form
